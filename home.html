About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I am a self-taught full-stack web developer and an environmental health sciences student. I combine my passion for technology and public health to solve real-world challenges. Below, you can explore my skills in both areas:

Programming Skills

  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript: Building responsive and dynamic websites.
  • Front-End Development: Creating interactive user interfaces and working with modern JavaScript frameworks like React and Vue.js.
  • Back-End Development: Developing APIs, working with Node.js, Express, and databases like MongoDB and MySQL.
  • Web Security: Implementing security best practices to safeguard websites and applications.
  • Version Control: Using Git for version control and GitHub for collaboration.

Environmental Health Skills

  • Environmental Health Risk Assessment: Identifying, evaluating, and managing environmental health risks related to air, water, soil, food, and chemical exposure.
  • Public Health Research: Conducting research on environmental health issues, including epidemiological studies and data analysis.
  • Toxicology and Environmental Monitoring: Understanding the impact of pollutants on human health and conducting regular environmental monitoring.
  • Waste Management: Developing and implementing sustainable waste management and sanitation practices.
  • Water and Food Safety: Ensuring the safety of drinking water and food through proper management and regulation.

With this combination of programming skills and environmental health expertise, I aim to contribute to building sustainable and health-conscious solutions for communities worldwide.
